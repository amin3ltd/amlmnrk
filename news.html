News and Job Vacancies

Latest News

October 2024: Rekik Trading PLC proudly announces the launch of our new eco-friendly detergent line, which utilizes biodegradable ingredients to ensure a cleaner environment. These products are designed to meet the growing demand for sustainable cleaning solutions without compromising on performance.

September 2024: We successfully concluded our annual business summit in Addis Ababa, where industry leaders gathered to discuss innovative practices in the detergent and soap manufacturing sectors. The event featured keynote speeches from prominent figures in sustainability and entrepreneurship.

August 2024: Rekik Trading PLC has been recognized as a top supplier of agricultural products at the Ethiopian National Trade Fair, showcasing our commitment to quality and service in the agricultural sector. We look forward to strengthening our partnerships with local farmers.

July 2024: Our community outreach program, "Clean for Change," successfully provided free cleaning supplies to schools across Addis Ababa, promoting hygiene and cleanliness among students. We believe in giving back to the community and supporting the next generation.

June 2024: We are excited to announce our partnership with international distributors to expand our product reach globally. This collaboration will allow us to introduce our high-quality detergents and soaps to new markets, further enhancing our brand presence worldwide.
