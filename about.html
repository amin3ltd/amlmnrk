SHORT HISTORY

REKIK TRADING PLC was established in 2023 with a vision to create impactful solutions across multiple sectors, including agriculture, security, real estate, and manufacturing. Founded by a team of visionary entrepreneurs, the company began its journey by addressing the needs of local communities through high-quality products and services. As the company grew, it expanded its offerings to include educational training, reflecting a commitment to not only enhancing businesses but also empowering individuals through knowledge and skill development

Over the years, REKIK TRADING PLC has built a reputation for reliability and excellence. The company has successfully executed numerous projects, adapting to changing market dynamics while maintaining a focus on sustainability and customer satisfaction. With a strong foundation rooted in integrity and quality, REKIK TRADING PLC continues to evolve, exploring new opportunities and innovations that contribute to the growth and development of its clients and the communities it serves.
